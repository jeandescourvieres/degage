🎯 Why don't we block "an entire carrier"?

Some competing apps claim to block, in bulk, numbers belonging to VoIP carriers (Aircall, Vonage, Manifone, Tata Communications, CM Telecom, etc.). Tu dégages made the opposite choice.

These carriers are general-purpose VoIP providers, not "spam carriers". They are used both by telemarketing call centers ❌ and by legitimate businesses — customer support, deliveries, medical appointments, tradespeople ✅. Blocking "all of Aircall" or "all of Vonage" also blocks these legitimate calls.

The right approach is the community database above: it captures numbers actually reported as spam, regardless of the carrier behind them — without ever penalizing legitimate uses of the same carrier.

Precise, reliable blocking rather than a blunt rejection made to impress.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Everything is also explained in the manual built into the app.

📞 I'm waiting for a callback from an unknown number (order, quote...): could it get blocked?

The risk is low: an unknown number is only blocked if it's in the spam database (known prefixes, community reports) or in one of your manual rules — not a typical customer service or delivery number.

If the call still gets blocked, it remains visible in History with the number and time: you can call back, then tap ✅ "Not spam" so it's never blocked again.

If you're expecting an important call and want zero risk, temporarily disable protection from the home screen during the call, then re-enable it afterwards.

👤 Can my contacts be blocked by mistake?

No. If a number calling you matches a contact saved on your phone, Tu dégages always lets the call through — even if that number is also in the spam database or in your custom rules.

✅ What if a number is blocked by mistake?

Go to the History tab, find the call in question and tap the ✅ "Not spam" icon. The number is immediately removed from your spam database and custom rules, and added to your local whitelist: it will never be blocked again — instant, offline, no email to send.
